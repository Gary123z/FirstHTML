Minimizing Impact Page

How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?

  1. Stop throwing out old computers
  2. Donate or sell unused computers
  3. There are some companys that take in unwanted computers

What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use?

  1. Turn off or unplug your computer when you aren't using it
  2. Optimize your computer to save the most power

How can we minimize the impact of computer device disposal on the environment?

  1. Stop dumping computers into landfills
  2. Recycle responsible
  3. Donate or sell unused computers
  4. Try to repair instead of getting a new one
